About TechnoWire

Technowire is a young and dynamic company with a passion for everything that has to do with ICT. Technowire has a goal to deliver information about the world of ICT as easy as possible for young adults, especially for people without a background or any knowledge about ICT. We want to put as much information as we can about ICT in one place on the internet so people will have easy access to the world of ICT.
